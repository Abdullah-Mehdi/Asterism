# Asterism Bot - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** The 21st of November, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Asterism ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information
- **Discord Server ID**: To identify where the bot is used
- **Discord Channel ID**: To know where to post updates
- **Command Usage**: When and which commands are used

### 2.2 User-Provided Information
- **AniList Usernames**: Provided via the `/track` command
- **AniList User IDs**: Retrieved from AniList's API

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
From AniList's public API:
- Activity data (anime/manga updates)
- User profile information (avatar, colors, title language preferences)
- Statistics (anime count, episodes watched, etc.)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Track and display AniList activity updates in Discord
- Store user preferences (profile colors, title languages)
- Provide statistics about tracked users
- Maintain and improve the bot's functionality

## 4. Data Storage

- Data is stored in a **SQLite database** hosted on Replit
- Data is retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data

## 5. Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes other than bot functionality

We **MAY** share data:
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety

## 6. Third-Party Services

This bot interacts with:
- **Discord**: Subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)
- **AniList**: Subject to [AniList's Privacy Policy](https://anilist.co/terms)
- **Replit**: Hosting provider, subject to [Replit's Privacy Policy](https://replit.com/site/privacy)

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request what data we have about you
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data using `/untrack`
- **Opt-out**: Remove the bot from your server at any time

## 8. Data Retention

- Tracking data is retained while a user is being tracked
- Data is deleted when `/untrack` is used
- Inactive data may be periodically cleaned

## 9. Children's Privacy

The bot does not knowingly collect information from users under 13. If you are under 13, do not use this bot.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
